You have a 100-year milestone, a rare and powerful differentiator, with no visual archive index, no searchable legacy gallery, and no named-athlete photography tied to it—meaning a prospective Saints recruit finds SPU's visual story before they find yours, and signs there instead.

This is not a photo shoot—it is a structured data deployment that turns 100 years of Saints history into compounding search equity your competitors cannot replicate.
